## City specifics

### Lima/Miraflores

!!! info
    [WikiTravel Lima](https://wikitravel.org/en/Lima) |
    [WikiTravel Lima/Miraflores]([https://wikitravel.org/en/Cuzco](https://wikitravel.org/en/Lima/Miraflores)) |
    [WikiVoyage Lima](https://en.wikivoyage.org/wiki/Lima) |
    [WikiVoyage Lima/Miraflores](https://en.wikivoyage.org/wiki/Lima/Miraflores)

Food:

- Chinese and Japanese, many roots/mixes with Peruvian foods

Specific meals/drinks to try:

- ceviche
- spicy, soury sauce, ask for aji
- Pisco Sour drink
- Jugos
- Chicha Morada

Places of interest:

- historic downtown
- Circuito Mágico del Agua
- Parque Kennedy
- Huaca Pucllana

Activities:

- Highland dances (Las Brisas del Titicaca), folkloric dance shows/festivals
- Peru Hop (inclusive tours)
- Bike tour
- shopping centres
- The Lima Gourmet Company, local market + cooking lessons etc
- Food tour Exquisito Perú
- Surfing!

### Cusco

!!! info
    [WikiTravel Cusco](https://wikitravel.org/en/Cuzco) |
    [WikiVoyage Cusco](https://en.wikivoyage.org/wiki/Cuzco)

Food:

- San Pedro
- Bagel Shop
- Organika
- Queenoa

Specific meals/drinks to try:

- Cuy
- Alpaca
- Cooked potatoes
- Chifa
- Inca Kola
- coca tea (coca leaves are the traditional native remedy for altitude sickness)

Hike:

- Salkantay
- Machu Picchu Jungle Trek
- Rainbow Mountain
- Colca Canyon
- Choquequirao

Places of interest:

- Coricancha temple
- fortress of Sacsayhuaman
- Ruins (many of them), like Sacsayhuaman
- salt mines of Moray

Activities:

- Horseback riding to Templo de la Luna and Zona X
- ATV/quadbike tour
- Sapo, a traditional bar game
- salsa class
- Chocolate Making Class
- Canoeing trip
- Morning markets in Juliaca and Puno
- Pisac, a town outside Cuzco, has a very big market

### Arequipa

!!! info
    [WikiTravel Arequipa](https://wikitravel.org/en/Arequipa) |
    [WikiVoyage Arequipa](https://en.wikivoyage.org/wiki/Arequipa)

Specific meals/drinks to try:

- chicharron
- Rocoto relleno con pastel de papa
- Alpaca steaks
- river shrimps, Chupe de Camarones
- spicy ingredient rocoto (Capsicum pubescens), example dish: rocoto relleno con pastel de papa

Places of interest:

- Plaza de Armas
- San Camilo Market
- Molino de Sabandía (Sabandía Mill)
- Cooking classes
- Downhill volcano biking or Volcano climbing
- Puente Bolognesi or Puente Fierro

Activities:

- sandboarding
- dune buggy drive
